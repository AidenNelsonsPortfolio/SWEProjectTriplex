How to play:

Start

    Start with pressing the spacebar, if the the ball goes outside of canvas then press space again.

Score

    When a ball collides with a brick, the score should increase based on the color.

Ball speed

    The ball should slowly get faster at certain points in the game

Sound

    Press the sound button if you want there to play sounds

Pause

    Press the escape key to pause the game
